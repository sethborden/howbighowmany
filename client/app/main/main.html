Super simple benchmarking.

Ever want to quickly ask a whole bunch of people "How many...", "How much...", or "How often..." type questions? We make doing that super easy. Just create a new benchmark below, share the link with whomever you want (or list it publicly) and start getting data. You'll be able to see the results graphed out just like the chart below. Check out some examples to get an idea of what you can do.

We're adding features on a regular basis, so feel free to contact us if there's something you'd like to see added.

Happy Benchmarking!
